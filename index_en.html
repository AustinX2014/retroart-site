Welcome to RETROART

A small indie studio located in Lanzarote in the Canary Islands Spain. Dedicated to the development of video games as well as multiple accessories.Founded in 2020.

"At RetroArt, we are driven by our deep passion for creating captivating video games that transport players into immersive and extraordinary worlds. Our team of talented developers and designers share a common enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation. Every day, we wake up inspired, fueled by the excitement of crafting memorable gaming experiences. The world of video games is our canvas, and we strive to weave intricate stories, breathtaking visuals, and addictive gameplay into each of our creations. What sets RetroArt apart is our unwavering dedication to quality and our commitment to delivering games that resonate with players on a profound level. We understand that gaming is not just a hobby; it's a way to escape, to connect, and to experience moments of pure joy. With every project we undertake, we embrace the challenges and channel our passion into turning visions into reality. We thrive on the thrill of bringing characters to life, designing captivating environments, and building engaging gameplay mechanics that captivate and challenge players. RetroArt's motivation lies in the belief that gaming has the power to inspire, entertain, and leave a lasting impact. We strive to create games that evoke emotions, spark imaginations, and leave players eagerly anticipating our next adventure. Join us on this incredible journey as we continue to pour our hearts and souls into crafting extraordinary video games that leave an indelible mark on the gaming industry."
